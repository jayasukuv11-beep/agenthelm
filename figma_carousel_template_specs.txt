# AgentHelm Figma Carousel Template Specs

## Dimensions
- **Frame**: 1080 x 1350 pixels (4:5 Portrait Ratio)
- **Safe Zone**: 150px padding on top/bottom to avoid UI overlap.

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: #FF4F00 (Industrial Signal Orange)
- **Background**: #0A0A0A (Pure Dark)
- **Surface**: #1A1A1A (Graphite Dark)
- **Accent**: #222222 (Border/Grid)

## Typography
- **Headlines**: Roboto Black Italic (Tracking: -0.05em, Line height: 0.9)
- **Body**: Inter Regular (Tracking: 0.01em, Line height: 1.5)
- **Labels**: Inter Bold (Uppercase, Tracking: 0.4em)

## Components
1. **Glass Card**: 
   - Fill: #FFFFFF (5% Opacity)
   - Background Blur: 20px
   - Border: 1px Stroke #FFFFFF (10% Opacity)
2. **Orange Glow**: 
   - Effect: Layer Blur 80px
   - Opacity: 30%
3. **Grain Texture**:
   - Overlay on top of all slides at 2% opacity for that "premium" noise.

## Export Instructions
- Format: PNG or JPG (High Quality)
- Sequence: Rename slides 01 to 08 for easy upload.
